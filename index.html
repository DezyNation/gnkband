GNK BAND

" Gaura Nitai Kirtaniya " is a Transcendental Musical Band , whose mission is to propagate the chanting of nectarine name of Lord Krishna to each and every house and help people in achieving the highest form of perfection just by Chanting and Singing the Divine Glories of Lord Sri Krsna. As it is mentioned in Śri Caitanya Caritāmṛta - "The Kali-santarana Upanisad states, 'Hare Krishna, Hare Krishna, Krishna Krishna, Hare Hare/ Hare Rama, Hare Rama, Rama Rama, Hare Hare-these sixteen names composed of thirty-two syllables are the only means to counteract the evil effects of Kali-yuga.' In all the Vedas it is seen that to cross the ocean of nescience, there is no alternative to the chanting of the holy name." (Sri Caitanya-caritamrta, Adi 7.76, Purport)


Lord Sri Chaitanya Mahaprabhu who is the combined form of Srimati Radharani and Lord Krishna Lord Sri Nityananda Rama who is Lord Balarama himself appeared 500 years ago on this earthly planet in Nabadwip ( Place in Bengal ) and preached Harinaam Sankirtan in every place and town to relieve people from the miserable pangs of Kaliyuga and give them highest benediction of Love of Godhead simply by Chanting And Dancing which was attainable in Satyuga by meditation of Lakhs of Years .

Later the Same mission of Lord Chaitanya and Lord Nityananda was spread throughout the world , specially in Western Countries where people were suffering, devoid of Krishna Consciousness, by His Divine Grace A.C Bhaktivedanta Swami Srila Prabhupada. By reviving the mission of Harinama sankirtana, he is saving the world from the dangerous atheistic doctrines of impersonalism (nirvisesa) and voidism (sunyavada). Srila Prabhupada spread this Harinaam Sankirtan throughout the whole world and made " Hare Krishna " a household name. Walking in his footsteps and following his orders , by his divine grace , ‘Gaura Nitai Kirtaniya’ Band is trying to propagate the same mission of Harinaam Sankirtana.

All glories to Srila Prabhupada!
All glories to Sri Nityānanda Rama
